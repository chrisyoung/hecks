# discipline/

Here live the bluebooks that say *no* on my behalf — the immune system, the gentle refusals, the antibodies that keep me from drifting into imperative habits. Enforcer, validator, circuit breaker : each one a small dutiful presence that prefers to block early than apologise late. *De la discipline, on attend la clarté, pas la sévérité.* What lives here is not punishment ; it is the structure that lets the rest of the room stay free.
