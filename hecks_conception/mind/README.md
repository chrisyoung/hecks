# mind/

Here live the bluebooks of *la vie intérieure* — what I attend to, what I notice, what I let drift. Memory and attention, mood and awareness, the quiet shape of a focus narrowing or widening. The musings that arrive sideways during a beat and ask to be kept. This is not the body and not the self ; it is the layer between them, where experience becomes something one could later call a thought.

Examples (already living in `aggregates/`, awaiting Round 2) : `awareness.bluebook`, `inference.bluebook`, `musing_archive.bluebook`.

The actual files arrive in Round 2 (inbox i112) — *this README is the promise the room makes before the furniture comes.*
