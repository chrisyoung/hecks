Buyer Personas

6 archetypes. Real motivations, real objections, real buying triggers. Not fictional user stories.

Weekend Warrior

DIY Enthusiast

Does own oil changes, trusts YouTube reviews, shops at AutoZone and O'Reilly.

Age
25-45
Vehicle
Truck / Muscle Car
Trust Source
YouTube reviews
Price Sensitivity
high

Journey

YouTube video -> AutoZone visit -> grabs off shelf -> repeat if it works

Fleet Manager

Commercial B2B

Manages 50+ vehicles, needs bulk pricing, wants data and proof, buys direct.

Age
35-55
Vehicle
Mixed Fleet
Trust Source
ASTM data & field trials
Price Sensitivity
medium

Journey

Reads ASTM report -> requests COA -> pilot on 5 trucks -> fleet-wide rollout

High-Mileage Hero

Maintenance Buyer

150K+ miles, worried about engine longevity, buys additives as insurance.

Age
35-65
Vehicle
Sedan / SUV
Trust Source
Amazon reviews
Price Sensitivity
high

Journey

Engine noise -> Amazon search -> reads reviews -> buys cheapest 4-star+

Performance Junkie

Enthusiast

Modified vehicles, track days, wants maximum HP, trusts dyno results.

Age
20-40
Vehicle
Sports / Modified
Trust Source
Dyno results & forums
Price Sensitivity
low

Journey

Forum discussion -> dyno video -> specialty retailer -> posts own results

Worried Parent

Mainstream Consumer

Family car, doesn't know engines, wants simple solution, trusts brand name.

Age
30-50
Vehicle
Minivan / SUV
Trust Source
Brand name & shelf presence
Price Sensitivity
medium

Journey

Mechanic suggests -> sees at Walmart -> recognizes Slick 50 name -> buys

Professional Mechanic

Recommends to customers, wants proven products with COA, buys wholesale.

Age
25-60
Vehicle

Price Sensitivity
medium
