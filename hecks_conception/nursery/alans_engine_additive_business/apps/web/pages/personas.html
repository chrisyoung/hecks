Buyer Personas

6 archetypes. Real motivations, real objections, real buying triggers.

Weekend Warrior

DIY Enthusiast

Does own oil changes, trusts YouTube reviews, shops at AutoZone and O'Reilly.

Age25-45
VehicleTruck / Muscle Car
Trust SourceYouTube reviews
Price Sensitivityhigh

Journey

Commercial B2B

Manages 50+ vehicles, needs bulk pricing, wants data and proof, buys direct.

Age35-55
VehicleMixed Fleet

Price Sensitivitymedium

Journey
